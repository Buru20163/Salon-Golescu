OUR STORY

Embracing the sublime pleasure of drink, the joy of good food, and the comfort of family – Salon Golescu is the embodiment of a passion for sharing the simple indulgences of life with friends. Just southeast of the steam clock in Gastown, Salon Golescu harkens back to century-old styles of indulgence and conviviality. The 100-year-old building, once home to the Leckie Boot Company since its construction in 1910, boasts Douglas fir ceilings, supported by large wooden pillars, and brick walls. Furnished with antiques from the era, complemented by custom creations, every piece in Salon Golescu tells a story. The centerpiece is a 38 ft. bar – meticulously handcrafted from 120-year-old planks of reclaimed Douglas fir. Entrance and hallway walls are adorned with era-inspired textures and gold leaf detailing – leaving no aesthetic untouched.

The cocktails at Salon Golescu have an old-fashioned soul, offered in both plain and fancy variations – they serve as the catalyst for the entire concept. Drawing inspiration from the turn of the century, the wine list is curated to encourage sharing and discovery. Beers cover a wide range of tastes and styles, with several selections showcasing the true ale craftsmanship of B.C. and the Pacific Northwest. Salon Golescu takes coffee seriously, serving a beautiful local roast and world-class quality loose-leaf tea – emphasizing quality over quantity, with a particular focus on whiskey and its applications in cocktails. The true essence of bartending shines through in the creation of personalized experiences.

The food at Salon Golescu is crafted with inspired and skillful techniques, rooted in familiar comforts. Hearty and mouth-watering meals, infused with a sense of familiarity, are prepared in a homemade style using fresh, local, and seasonal ingredients. We eagerly anticipate the opportunity to share our zest for life and appetite for indulgence with you. Salon Golescu embraces a singularly essential part of Vancouver – its own history.
